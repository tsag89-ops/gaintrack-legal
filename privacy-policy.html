Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 20, 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data GainTrack collects, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service.

Who We Are

GainTrack is a fitness tracking application developed and maintained by GainTrack. If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at gaintrack.fitness@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and data generated by your use of the app:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and profile picture via Google Sign-In
  • Fitness data: Workout logs, exercises, sets, reps, weight, and duration
  • Nutrition data: Meal entries, food items, and daily macro totals
  • Body measurements: Weight, body fat percentage, and physical measurements you choose to log
  • Goals and preferences: Fitness goals, equipment, calorie and macro targets
  • Usage data: Feature interactions and app performance telemetry
  • Push notification token: To deliver scheduled reminders (only if you grant permission)

How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to provide and improve GainTrack:

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your account
  • Store and sync your workout and nutrition history
  • Generate personalized progression suggestions and AI coaching responses
  • Send lifecycle notifications and reminders you opt into
  • Analyze aggregate usage to improve app features
  • Process subscription purchases via RevenueCat

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

AI Coach Feature

GainTrack Pro includes an AI coaching feature powered by Google Gemini. When you use this feature, your fitness data and the content of your messages are sent to our backend server and then to Google's Gemini API to generate responses. This data is used solely to produce your personalized response and is not used to train AI models.

Third-Party Services

GainTrack uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase & Firestore — authentication and cloud data storage
  • RevenueCat — subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Google Gemini API — AI coaching responses (Pro feature)
  • Expo / EAS — app delivery and over-the-air updates

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) and MongoDB Atlas, both of which use industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. We use Firebase Authentication to ensure only you can access your account. We do not store passwords — authentication is handled entirely by Google Sign-In.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our systems within 30 days. Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained longer.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time via device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at gaintrack.fitness@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

GainTrack is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version.
